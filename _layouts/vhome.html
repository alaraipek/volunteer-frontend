Connecting Volunteers to Opportunities

Connecting volunteering organizations to students in an easy and accessible way.

Through filters, users can pick interests and hobbies to find volunteering opportunities that align with their interests.

Different filters, based on past experience, age and location allow the user can match with volunteering opportunities that are perfect for them!

We will also include details about each volunteering organization, what they are looking for and their application process.


Why We Chose This

As high schoolers who are required to do a minimum amount of volunteer hours, it's often hard to find volunteer opportunities that align with our interests. A website that is easy to navigate and gives us access to different volunteering opportunities will encourage people to volunteer more. I think this website could be very useful for many people in our school who are looking for different opportunities.
